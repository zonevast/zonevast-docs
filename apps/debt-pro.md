# DebtPro

## Purpose
Debt and credit management with analytics and reporting.

## Key Features
- Debt tracking and management
- Payment plans
- Credit analysis
- Debt collection
- Payment processing
- Analytics and reports (D3.js)
- Debt aging reports

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 14 (App Router)
- D3.js (data visualization)
- @tanstack/react-query
- @nextui-org/react
- next-intl
- GraphQL
